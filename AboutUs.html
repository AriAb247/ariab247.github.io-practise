About Us

Mission Statement

Altum Medical Ltd. is dedicated to reshaping the future of healthcare by integrating innovative technology with traditional medical infrastructure. Our immediate mission is to enhance patient safety and increase service efficiency within the NHS and beyond, through the development of a novel devices such as our handheld bedside blood labelling device, that automates much of the pre-analytical phase of blood sampling. Designed to reduce labelling errors, it is the first step towards our broader vision.


Ultimately, our aspiration is to cultivate a holistic healthcare ecosystem that harmonises seamlessly with the NHS, fusing public health values with the dynamism and efficacy of private enterprise. We envision administering a network of GP clinics, hospitals, pharmacies, surgeries and others, providing a comprehensive range of healthcare services. This expansive infrastructure will generate a wealth of data, which we will leverage to consistently refine our services and propel healthcare advancements.


Unlike traditional healthcare companies, our solutions are uniquely tailored, harnessing the potential of modern technology to meet the intricate needs of today's healthcare industry. Our blend of medical knowledge, technological prowess, and a passion for improving patient outcomes set us apart, making us not just a healthcare company, but a pioneer in the new era of health-tech. We do not want to simply to follow the current path of healthcare, but to innovate, advance and ultimately, reshape its future for the betterment of all.
